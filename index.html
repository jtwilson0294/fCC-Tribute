Wernher von Braun

The original RocketMan

von Braun was a German and American Aerospace Engineer and Space Architect who lived from 1912 to 1977

Overview of von Braun's life

  • In his twenties and early thirties, von Braun worked in Nazi Germany's rocket development program.
  • Following the war, he was secretly moved to the United States, along with about 1,600 other German scientists, engineers, and technicians, as part of Operation Paperclip.
  • His group was assimilated into NASA, where he served as director of the newly formed Marshall Space Flight Center
  • In 1975, von Braun received the National Medal of Science.
  • He advocated a human mission to Mars.
